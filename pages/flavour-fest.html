Flavour Fest 2022

What is Flavour Fest?

Flavour fest, is an event which takes place throughout the month of June 2022. People come together to celebrate and bond within their communities. Additionally, it allows local businesses to flourish as they have a platform to advertise their products and services. Flavour fest falls under the Vision 2024 project and aims to celebrate diversity and culture within the Calderdale region by maximising opportunities for locals which they can enjoy.

Where is Flavour Fest Located?

Flavour fest is located all around the Calderdale region with multiple events taking place over the month of june. Locations, Dates and Times will be displayed on here and will ensure all users are up-to-date on when and where the next event takes place.

Flavour fest is a month-long festival in June 2022 organised and funded by the Inclusive Economy Project and featuring many community-based events and activities in the Park and Warley area. Additionally, it ties in with the vision 2024 project which aims to celebrate diversity and culture in Calderdale maximising opportunities for local people to enjoy, learn and experience a larger life and promoting Park and Warley as a great place to live, work and visit
