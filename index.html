Flavourfest 2022

Explore, Discover, Experience

Flavourfest is a month-long celebration of culture and diversity this June in one of the most vibrant and distinctive places in Calderdale.

The festival programme includes events and activities across the Calderdale wards of Park and Warley (also known asWest Central Halifax) for people to enjoy, learn and experience a larger life.

Flavour fest is a month-long festival in June 2022 organised and funded by the Inclusive Economy Project and featuring many community-based events and activities in the Park and Warley area. Additionally, it ties in with the vision 2024 project which aims to celebrate diversity and culture in Calderdale maximising opportunities for local people to enjoy, learn and experience a larger life and promoting Park and Warley as a great place to live, work and visit
